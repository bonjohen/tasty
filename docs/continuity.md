## Character State

### Hurkuzak
- **Physical:** Dramatically improved from the consumption. Stronger, warmer, more alert than he should be. Enhanced senses (distance vision, hearing). Cold resistance. Grip strength increased. Body answering to different rules.
- **Injuries:** All previous injuries healed. No current wounds. Healing rate dramatically beyond normal.
- **Knowledge:** Has discovered the flesh economy is systemic (Ch 26). Suspects Hakiia knew about the system. Has returned to the wreck site and recovered Hakiia's hidden cache: three ceramic containers (contents unknown), ritual/anatomical diagrams, lists of materials matching flesh-economy preparations, notes on sibling consumption mechanism ("inheritance"), and correspondence fragments referencing a "vessel" in transactional/logistical language from an unknown hand. Has not yet fully processed the implications — comprehension is lagging behind the evidence. Has hatchet, knife, bow, provisions from Merrath, plus the recovered materials.
- **The Bleed stage:** Middle-to-late. NEW (Ch 28-29): At the cliff site, voice went dense and charged — intense nonverbal pressure, recognition from another angle (Ch 28). During the wreck search (Ch 29), voice reacted to the sealed containers with sharp recognition — it knew these objects as things it had touched. When Hurkuzak read the papers (ritual diagrams, sibling consumption notes, correspondence), the voice escalated to panic-level pressure: weight behind eyes, chest, base of skull. Not words but something like a man watching his own diary read aloud. By chapter's end, voice is a wordless weight — unreadable. Could be grief, guilt, or the energy of something caught.
- **Emotional state:** Reconciliation from Sec 11 is intact but about to collapse. Has found evidence that Hakiia packed ritual materials, studied the sibling consumption mechanism, and received transactional correspondence referencing a "vessel." Comprehension is lagging behind the evidence — body shaking, mind circling. Has not yet landed on the full implication (that Hakiia intended to kill and consume him). That realization belongs to Section 14. Currently sitting on the ridge above the ravine with evidence spread before him, about to read in earnest.

### Hakiia
- **Status:** Dead. Killed by Hurkuzak in a mercy killing (Ch 9). Consent was explicit, lucid, and unquestionable.
- **Physical at death:** Both legs broken, right leg dying from infection/necrosis. Gash across forehead. Left shoulder injury. Right leg tissue dead from ankle upward. Body moved to cold alcove in the cabin (Ch 12). Preserved by cold. Furs over the face.
- **Final words/actions:** Said "please" — first time in his life. Acknowledged the cost to Hurkuzak ("I know that the weight of it does not end when my weight ends"). Said he trusted Hurkuzak to carry it. Gripped Hurkuzak's wrist at the moment of the knife — held on, then let go. Eyes stayed open after death; Hurkuzak closed them.
- **Voice presence:** N/A (alive)
- **Key memories surfaced:** N/A

### Captain Sella Vark
- **Physical:** Healthy. Stronger, faster, and more resilient than a normal human. Better night vision. Heals in days rather than weeks. Can work in extreme cold. All from years of consuming demon flesh in measured amounts.
- **Location:** Cassavon, a trade town. Rents a stone workspace behind a tanner's yard for processing.
- **Knowledge of Hurkuzak:** Has tracked him, investigated the bandit fight, shot him with a crossbow bolt, and observed his healing. Confirmed: capabilities exceed standard demon range. Healing factor is a multiple of the best she's seen. Blood scent is mixed (not standard demon, not human). Movement profile shows "installed" rather than inherent capabilities. Working hypothesis: sibling consumption. Does not know his name or specific history. Has a blood sample. Pursuing at professional distance.
- **Consumed properties:** Enhanced strength (folds heavy demon hide one-handed), speed, healing, cold resistance, night vision. Changes accumulated over years of professional consumption.
- **Professional position:** Respected hunter, best in her range. Works with factor Koss (southern trading house). Understands the system's hierarchy — she is the hands, the profit goes to those above her. Not bitter about it; exact.
