## Character State

### Hurkuzak
- **Physical:** Dramatically improved from the consumption. Stronger, warmer, more alert than he should be. Enhanced senses (distance vision, hearing). Cold resistance. Grip strength increased. Body answering to different rules.
- **Injuries:** All healed or healing rapidly. Wolf bite closed over in days (unusual speed). Ribs mended. Knee functional. Palm scar thinned. The healing rate is the first mechanical sign of the consumption's effects.
- **Knowledge:** Cabin ahead, direction known but distance uncertain. Has hatchet, knife, skinning tools, rope, limited dried meat, two furs, travois. Understands the basin is a trap — cliff blocks retreat, dead dogs hold wolves temporarily, wolves will come for them once the easy meat is gone.
- **The Bleed stage:** Not begun. No inner voice. All thoughts are his own.
- **Emotional state:** Has killed his brother and carried his body to the cabin. The mercy and the killing are both permanently true. During the march, Hakiia's body changed categories — from brother to weight to burden. Considered leaving the body; could not. Spoke aloud to Hakiia's absence ("You were right about the leg. You were wrong about the rest."). Collapsed on the cabin floor beside the body. Unconscious. Something in him destroyed. The next story begins when he wakes.

### Hakiia
- **Status:** Dead. Killed by Hurkuzak in a mercy killing (Ch 9). Consent was explicit, lucid, and unquestionable.
- **Physical at death:** Both legs broken, right leg dying from infection/necrosis. Gash across forehead. Left shoulder injury. Right leg tissue dead from ankle upward. Body moved to cold alcove in the cabin (Ch 12). Preserved by cold. Furs over the face.
- **Final words/actions:** Said "please" — first time in his life. Acknowledged the cost to Hurkuzak ("I know that the weight of it does not end when my weight ends"). Said he trusted Hurkuzak to carry it. Gripped Hurkuzak's wrist at the moment of the knife — held on, then let go. Eyes stayed open after death; Hurkuzak closed them.
- **Voice presence:** N/A (alive)
- **Key memories surfaced:** N/A

### Captain Sella Vark
- **Physical:** Healthy. Stronger, faster, and more resilient than a normal human. Better night vision. Heals in days rather than weeks. Can work in extreme cold. All from years of consuming demon flesh in measured amounts.
- **Location:** Cassavon, a trade town. Rents a stone workspace behind a tanner's yard for processing.
- **Knowledge of Hurkuzak:** None. Has heard rumors of a premium buyer seeking fresh mountain-range demon stock from the eastern passes — this will eventually point her toward the brothers' region.
- **Consumed properties:** Enhanced strength (folds heavy demon hide one-handed), speed, healing, cold resistance, night vision. Changes accumulated over years of professional consumption.
- **Professional position:** Respected hunter, best in her range. Works with factor Koss (southern trading house). Understands the system's hierarchy — she is the hands, the profit goes to those above her. Not bitter about it; exact.
